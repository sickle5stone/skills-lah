# 📋 The skills-lah Wishlist

This is a live, community-driven wishlist of **highly practical Singapore-focused skills, unwritten rules, and app-specific hacks** we want to document in this repository. 

If you have firsthand experience with any of these items, please consider opening a Pull Request (PR) to share your knowledge with the community, lah! Even a short guide or a few bullet points helps tremendously.

---

## 🛠️ The Skills (Practical Know-How)

### 💼 Career & Government Subsidies
* [ ] **1. The SkillsFuture / UTAP "Free Course" Arbitrage**
  * *What to document:* The exact workflow to stack your standard $500 SkillsFuture credits with NTUC’s UTAP (Union Training Assistance Programme) to get high-tier professional certs or specialized classes down to literally $0 or pocket change out of pocket.
* [ ] **2. Driving License Direct-Booking Hacking (BBDC / CDC / SSDC)**
  * *What to document:* The exact refresh times, bot-beating strategies, and forum hacks required to book practical driving slots and tests without waiting 8 months for an opening.

### 🏦 Financial Engineering & Housing
* [ ] **3. The CPF Ordinary Account (OA) to Special Account (SA) Transfer Math**
  * *What to document:* The exact cut-off ages, compounding math, and psychological pros/cons of moving your OA funds to SA early for that guaranteed 4% return, balancing it against your future HDB downpayment needs.
* [ ] **4. The Supplementary Retirement Scheme (SRS) Tax Bracket Drop**
  * *What to document:* How to calculate the exact amount of cash to park in an SRS account before December 31st to legally drop yourself into a lower income tax bracket, and how to avoid letting that cash sit idle rotting to 0.05% interest.
* [ ] **5. HDB Grant Stacking & Loan-to-Value (LTV) Optimization**
  * *What to document:* Navigating the maze of the Enhanced Housing Grant (EHG) and Proximity Housing Grant (PHG) without leaving tens of thousands of dollars on the table when booking a BTO or negotiating a resale flat.

### 🍱 Local Culture & Everyday Fluency
* [ ] **6. Hawker Table Chope Culture & Social Etiquette**
  * *What to document:* The unwritten laws of CBD lunch hours. Managing the tissue packet tier list, understanding queue dynamics at legendary stalls, and how to order your mixed rice (Cai Fan) without getting penalized by the "fish price" trap.
* [ ] **7. Kopi Ordering Vocabulary Mastery**
  * *What to document:* A definitive, foolproof phonetic guide to ordering traditional Nanyang coffee exactly how you want it (*Kopi C Kosong Gao*, *Kopi O Siew Dai*, etc.) without panicking when the uncle stares you down.

### 🏥 Health, Wellness & Public Safety Net
* [ ] **8. Mild Trauma & Muscle Recovery Pathways**
  * *What to document:* Navigating local recovery methods without breaking the bank. Knowing when to look for specialized Western systems (like BASI Pilates rehabilitative movement) versus traditional local Chinese Tui Na practitioners for chronic desk-job back pain.
* [ ] **9. Polyclinic to Specialist Referral Hacking**
  * *What to document:* How to systematically navigate the public healthcare system to get subsidized specialist care or MRI scans without paying private hospital premium rates, including the exact wait-time tradeoffs.
* [ ] **10. CDC Voucher Maxing & Household Pooling**
  * *What to document:* How to efficiently claim, split, and pool digital CDC vouchers across family members, and finding the specific heartland merchants (beyond just Giant and NTUC) that offer extra discounts when you spend them.

## 🤝 How to Claim a Wishlist Item

1. Look through the list above and find a topic you know well.
2. Open an **Issue** with the title `[Wishlist Claim] Topic Name` so others know you are working on it.
3. Write your guide in markdown, place it in the appropriate folder (e.g., `/skills` or `/apps`), and submit a **Pull Request**. 

*Don't see a crucial skill or app that deserves to be here? Add it to this list via a PR!*
